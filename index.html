Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and platform.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our website by clicking on the "Book Appointment" button and filling out the form. Alternatively, you can call our office during business hours to schedule an appointment with our staff.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please check with your insurance provider to confirm coverage for telemedicine services. You can also contact our billing department for assistance with insurance questions.

How does virtual consultation work?

After booking a virtual consultation, you'll receive a confirmation email with a link to join the video call at your scheduled time. You'll need a device with a camera, microphone, and internet connection. Our platform works on computers, tablets, and smartphones.

Can I get a prescription through online consultation?

Yes, our licensed physicians can prescribe medications when appropriate during a virtual consultation. Prescriptions are sent electronically to your preferred pharmacy. However, certain controlled substances may require an in-person visit as per regulations.

What if I need emergency care?

For life-threatening emergencies, please call 233551633907 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately. Our virtual services are not suitable for emergency situations that require immediate, in-person medical attention.
